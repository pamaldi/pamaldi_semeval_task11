### Common fallacies (INVALID):

- Undistributed middle: Middle term not distributed in either premise
- Illicit major: Major term distributed in conclusion but not in major premise
- Illicit minor: Minor term distributed in conclusion but not in minor premise
- Two negative premises: Cannot derive valid conclusion from two negatives
- Negative premise with affirmative conclusion: Invalid
- Two particular premises: Cannot derive valid conclusion

### Distribution rules:
- A (All S are P): S is distributed, P is not
- E (No S is P): Both S and P are distributed
- I (Some S are P): Neither is distributed
- O (Some S are not P): Only P is distributed
